ABOTURIBO

“BYUKA RABAGIRANA” ni ihuriro ry’abakristukazi baturuka mu madini
n’amatorero atandukanye,Rikaba ryaratangiye muri 07/2020. Abo bakristukazi biyemeje kwamamaza inkuru nziza ya Yesu Kristu mu bikorwa byo gutabara no gutabariza abashavuye haba ku mutima no ku mubiri. Ibikorwa byabo bigamije kwita cyane cyane ku bategarugori n’abari bakeneye ubutabazi butandukanye igihe bageze mu ngorane badafite umuntu wo kubitaho (intabwa cg incike). Mu butumwa bwacu tuzibanda ku byiciro dusanga muri Bibiliya mu magambo Umwami wacu Yezu/Yesu yavuze ubwo yagiraga ati: “..…kuko nari nshonje mukamfungurira, nari mfite inyota mumpa icyo kunywa,
nari umushyitsi murancumbikira, nari nambaye ubusa muranyambika, nari ndwaye muransura, nari mu nzu y’imbohe muza kundeba”. (Mt 25: 35-36).

Intego rusange

Intego y’abagize Organization “BYUKA URABAGIRANE” ni uguhumuriza abari n’abategarugori baruhijwe n’ibibazo bitandukanye no kubagarurira icyizere cy’ubuzima.

01

Kwita ku bagore/abakobwa bageze mu ngorane (uburwayi, gufungwa, gutabw kubura amahoro y’umutima biturutse ku mpamvu iyo ariyo yose) badafite uwo batabaza;

02

Guteganya ahantu hizewe kandi hatekanye hazaba hagenewe kwakira uwaba yifuza uwa amatwi ngo amubwire agahinda ke cyangwa akeneye uwamuyobora mu gihe ahuye n’ingorane;

03

Guhugura buri wese wifuza ubumenyi cyangwa ubwunganizi mu bijyanye n’ubutumwa bwo gutega amatwi umuntu ufite ibibazo.

Turi Umuryango Mugari

Buri wese mu bagize Organization agomba kuba asobanukiwe ko ubutumwa asabwa gusohoza yabuhawe n’uwamuhamagaye (YESU/YEZU KRISTO); bityo inkomoko y’ubushobozi bwose izaturuka mu kumutega amatwi (isengesho mbere ya byose) no kwemera kumvira (gukora icyo abwiwe n’Imana Yo ubwayo cyangwa ibinyujije mu bamukuriye).
